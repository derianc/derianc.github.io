Delete Your BarPals Account

To delete your BarPals account and all associated data:

  1. Open the BarPals app on your mobile device.
  2. Go to Settings → Profile → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

All personal information will be permanently deleted from our systems within 90 days, except where retention is required by law.

If you encounter issues, contact us at support@barpals.app.
